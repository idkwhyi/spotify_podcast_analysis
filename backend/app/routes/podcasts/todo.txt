Podcast Table Routes
GET /podcasts: Retrieve all podcasts with optional filters (e.g., by region, date, rank).
GET /podcasts/:showURI: Retrieve details of a specific podcast by showURI.
POST /podcasts: Add a new podcast.
PUT /podcasts/:showURI: Update details of a podcast by showURI.
DELETE /podcasts/:showURI: Delete a podcast by showURI.
