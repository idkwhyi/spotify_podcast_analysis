Episode Table Routes
GET /episodes: Retrieve all episodes with optional filters (e.g., by podcast, region, date, rank).
GET /episodes/:episodeURI: Retrieve details of a specific episode by episodeURI.
POST /episodes: Add a new episode.
PUT /episodes/:episodeURI: Update details of an episode by episodeURI.
DELETE /episodes/:episodeURI: Delete an episode by episodeURI.
